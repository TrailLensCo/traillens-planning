## NOTES FOR DOCUMENT CREATION

### Key Differences: Comprehensive Plan vs. Executive Overview

**Comprehensive Business Plan (40-60 pages)**:
- Detailed analysis and supporting data
- Complete financial models
- Extensive competitive analysis
- Full risk assessment
- Comprehensive appendices
- Designed for thorough due diligence
- Used by banks, government agencies, serious investors

**Executive Overview (4-5 pages)**:
- High-level summary
- Key metrics only
- Standalone document
- Quick evaluation tool
- Used for initial meetings, introductions

### Writing Approach

1. **Start with existing documents**: Use FINANCIAL_PLAN.md, PRODUCT_OVERVIEW_FOR_CEO.md, MARKETING_PLAN.md, etc. as source material
2. **Expand and enhance**: Add context, analysis, and supporting detail
3. **Maintain consistency**: Ensure all numbers and facts align across sections
4. **Add visual elements**: Charts, graphs, tables to illustrate key points
5. **Professional tone**: Formal business writing suitable for banks and investors
6. **Evidence-based**: Every claim supported by data or credible source
7. **Conservative assumptions**: Err on the side of caution in projections
