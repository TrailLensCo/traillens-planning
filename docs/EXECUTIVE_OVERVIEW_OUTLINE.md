# TrailLensHQ Executive Overview Outline
## 4-5 Page Standalone Business Summary

**Document Purpose**: Standalone executive overview for rapid evaluation by banks, government agencies, and potential investors. This document is designed to be read independently without requiring the full comprehensive business plan.

**Target Length**: 4-5 pages (1,200-1,500 words)

**Target Audience**:
- Commercial bank loan officers
- Government grant reviewers (IRAP, SR&ED)
- Angel investors and early-stage VCs

- Board members requiring quick updates

## Notes on Distinction: Executive Overview vs. Executive Summary

**Executive Overview** (this document): A standalone 4-5 page document designed to be read independently. Contains sufficient detail for decision-making without requiring the full business plan. Used for:
- Initial investor meetings
- Bank loan applications
- Government grant submissions
- Board updates
- Partnership discussions

**Executive Summary** (within comprehensive business plan): A 1-2 page summary that appears at the beginning of the full 40-60 page business plan. Summarizes the full plan and assumes the reader will read the complete document for details.

The Executive Overview is more comprehensive and can stand alone; the Executive Summary is a condensed teaser for the full plan.
